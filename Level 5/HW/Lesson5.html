Sonny from A Bronx Tale

Sonny is a character from the movie "A Bronx Tale," directed by Robert De Niro.

Description

Sonny is a Mafia boss in the Bronx during the 1960s. He's powerful and respected.

Role

Sonny mentors the main character, C, teaching him about loyalty and respect.

Relationship with C

Sonny takes care of C and teaches him life lessons, but also exposes him to the dangers of the streets.

Impact

Sonny's guidance helps C understand the complexities of life in their neighborhood.
